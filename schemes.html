Government EV Incentives & Schemes

🏛️ FAME-II Scheme (National)

2-Wheeler: ₹15,000 per vehicle

3-Wheeler: ₹15,000 per vehicle

4-Wheeler (Private): ₹1,50,000 per vehicle (max vehicle price ₹15 lakhs)

Commercial Vehicles: Major subsidy available

Covers battery cost up to 40% for 2-wheelers and 30% for 4-wheelers

⚡ Income Tax Benefits

Section 32(1)(vi): 100% deduction on cost of EV

Faster depreciation: For commercial vehicles

No benefit recapture: On resale of EV

Makes EV more affordable for business and individual buyers

💰 GST Benefits

5% GST: On battery pack supply separately

12% GST: On complete vehicle (reduced from 28-48%)

Zero customs duty: On EV charging equipment

Significantly reduces overall vehicle cost

🚗 Road Tax & Registration Benefits

Exemption/Reduction: In multiple states (up to 100% exemption)

Fast-track registration: Priority processing

Lower insurance: Reduced premium in some states

Varies by state - check local regulations

State-Wise EV Incentives

🌃 Maharashtra

2-Wheeler: ₹10,000

4-Wheeler: ₹1,50,000

Road Tax: 100% exemption

Registration Fee: 50% waived for first 10,000 EVs

One of India's most generous EV incentive schemes

🏙️ Delhi

2-Wheeler: ₹15,000 + road tax waiver

4-Wheeler: ₹1,50,000

VAT Relief: 5% reduction on purchase

Charging Stations: Free installation support

100% road tax exemption on all EVs

🤖 Karnataka

2-Wheeler: Up to ₹10,000

4-Wheeler: Up to ₹1,00,000

Road Tax: 100% exemption

Special Features: Tax holiday for EV manufacturers

Fast-growing EV hub in South India

🌄 Andhra Pradesh

Four-Wheeler: Up to ₹90,000

Road Tax & Registration: 100% waived

EV Charging Infrastructure: Government support

Manufacturing Incentives: For EV industry

Strong focus on EV manufacturing ecosystem

🏖️ Tamil Nadu

2-Wheeler: ₹25,000

4-Wheeler: Up to ₹2,00,000

Road Tax: 100% exemption

Charging Infrastructure: Free public charging

Highest subsidy among Indian states

🏜️ Rajasthan

2-Wheeler: ₹30,000

3-Wheeler: ₹5,00,000

4-Wheeler: ₹1,00,000

Road Tax: Exemption for 5-8 years

Very attractive for commercial vehicle operators

🚄 Telangana

2-Wheeler: ₹10,000

4-Wheeler: Up to ₹75,000

Road Tax: 100% exemption

Special Zones: For EV manufacturing

Tech hub with focus on EV adoption

🌴 Kerala

4-Wheeler: Up to ₹1,50,000

Road Tax: 50% reduction

Insurance Premium: Partially subsidized

Charging Infrastructure: Government support

Focus on sustainable mobility solutions

💡 Important Notes:

  • Eligibility: Schemes vary by vehicle type, price, and buyer category
  • Documentation: Keep purchase invoices, registration, and subsidy forms
  • Timing: Some schemes have limited daily/monthly quotas - apply early
  • Combine Benefits: National + State + Tax benefits can add up significantly
  • Updates: Check official government websites for latest scheme changes
  • State Transfers: Eligibility may change if you move to another state
